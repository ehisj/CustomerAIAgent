Acme Corp Company Policies

RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

General Returns:
- All products can be returned within 30 days of delivery date
- Items must be in original packaging with all accessories included
- Original receipt or order confirmation required
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after inspection

Exceptions:
- Electronics: Must be returned within 15 days, unopened or defective
- Personalized items: Non-returnable unless defective
- Clearance items marked "Final Sale": Non-returnable
- Gift cards: Non-refundable

Defective Products:
- Defective items can be exchanged or refunded within the warranty period
- Contact customer support for a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
- We cover return shipping costs for defective items

Refund Methods:
- Original payment method receives refund
- Store credit available upon request (bonus 10% added to store credit refunds)
- Cash purchases refunded by check

WARRANTY INFORMATION

Standard Warranty Coverage:
- All products include manufacturer warranty
- Warranty period varies by product (see product page for details)
- Covers defects in materials and workmanship
- Does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications

Extended Warranty (Acme Care):
- Available for purchase at checkout
- Extends coverage to 3 years from purchase date
- Includes accidental damage protection
- One-time screen replacement for applicable devices
- Priority customer support
- Price: 15% of product price

How to File a Warranty Claim:
1. Contact customer support with proof of purchase
2. Describe the issue in detail
3. Receive RMA number if replacement/repair is needed
4. Ship item to our service center (we provide prepaid label)
5. Receive repaired item or replacement within 7-10 business days

SHIPPING POLICY

Processing Time:
- Orders placed before 2 PM ET ship same business day
- Orders placed after 2 PM ET ship next business day
- Custom or personalized items: 3-5 business days processing

Shipping Options (Continental US):
- Standard (5-7 days): Free on orders over $75, otherwise $5.99
- Express (2-3 days): $12.99
- Next Day: $24.99 (order by 2 PM ET)

Alaska, Hawaii, and Territories:
- Standard: $12.99 (7-10 days)
- Express: $24.99 (3-5 days)

International Shipping:
- Available to 50+ countries
- Rates calculated at checkout based on destination and weight
- Customs fees and duties are customer responsibility
- Delivery: 7-14 business days

Lost or Damaged Packages:
- Contact us within 48 hours of expected delivery
- We will file a claim and send replacement or refund
- Photo evidence may be required for damaged items
