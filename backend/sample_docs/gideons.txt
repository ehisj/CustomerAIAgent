NEED BIBLES?
============

OUR UNIQUE APPROACH
-------------------

The Gideons International is an association of Christian business and professional men and their wives committed to transforming lives through the Gospel of Jesus Christ. While known for placing Bibles in hotel rooms, Scripture distribution is one part of their broader mission to help members share the Good News.

The organization strategically places God's Word in locations where people often seek hope, believing that Scripture can "plant seeds that the Lord will grow in His perfect timing" and offers a lasting witness to truth.

HOW THEY CAN HELP
-----------------

Bible Placement at Facilities:
Members place complimentary Scripture copies in hotels, hospitals, and other facilities. Organizations can contact them to request placements.

Friends of The Gideons:
Supporters gain access to pocket-sized Scriptures and resources to strengthen witnessing skills through prayer and financial support.

Gideon Bible App:
Offers the Old and New Testaments with audio options in over 2,200 language translations, plus verse helps and notes.

ABOUT THE GIDEONS INTERNATIONAL
===============================

MISSION STATEMENT
-----------------

The Gideons International is an evangelical association dedicated to equipping Christian business and professional men, along with their wives, to share God's Word and win others for Jesus Christ worldwide.

CORE PURPOSE
------------

Their single mission involves three key activities:
- Strengthening one another through fellowship
- Sharing the Gospel message
- Distributing God's Word in hotels, hospitals, shelters, and other locations where people seek hope

-----------------

GLOBAL IMPACT
-------------

- Over 2.6 billion people reached worldwide
- More than 240,000 active Gideons and Auxiliary members
- Operates in approximately 12,000 areas globally

HISTORICAL HIGHLIGHTS
---------------------

Founded in 1899 by John Nicholson, Samuel Hill, and W.J. Knights, the organization has achieved numerous milestones:

- 1899: The Gideons International founded
- 1908: First Hotel Bible placement in Montana
- 1934: Women's Auxiliary established
- 1941: Service Testaments distributed to Pearl Harbor personnel
- 2001: One billionth Scripture distributed
- 2024: International Scripture blitz in Cambodia

VALUES
------

The organization emphasizes that their work serves as "an extended missionary arm of the church," rooted in the biblical principle that God's Word endures eternally.
